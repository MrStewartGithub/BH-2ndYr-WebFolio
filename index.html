Really Neat Content!

In the world of Emotus, heroes of all shapes and sizes emerge from the jaws of obscurity and become honoured, championed, legends! Then there’s your character. Start out as a complete nobody with a big heart, to a complete nobody with a big heart who punches evil stuff! Forge your own pathway in this strange new world, explore the land and meet the strange citizens of the land, protect them from those who seek to disrupt the peace, or leave them be and guarantee your safety. What kind of hero will you be?

There are those in the land who seek power in many ways, some through honest work and heroism, but in acts of sheer desperation, many turn to darker magic. Twisted by feverish hate, these lost souls wander the land aimless, attacking those unfortunate enough to cross paths. Many heroes accept the call to action due to the untold riches those may claim by slaying one of these foul creatures. Although, it does beg the question, what is the origin of such disturbing creatures?

Emotions. The very essence of a person. Thousands of years ago humanity discovered that through feeling an emotion so intense and passionate, they can conjure powerful spells, mighty apparitions, and bend the very shape of reality. Or you know, do fairly impressive magic tricks at parties. Most larger societies beyond small villages believe that the true path to power is to dedicate ones own life into one of the core 8 emotions. You however, hail from a quaint river cottage. Having never heard of Emotion Magic before, you can discover the complex world of Emotus through your character’s eyes!
